Marry-Me Chicken

Home

Description

Marry-me chicken is one of my favorite pasta recipes to make with chicken. It creates a rich and creamy sauce that tenderizes the chicken wonderfully. Furthermore, if cooked correctly, a proposal may be imminent due to the deliciousness of the meal.

Ingredients

  • 1.5 Lbs boneless skinless chicken breast halves
  • 2 Tbsp butter
  • 3 minced cloves garlic
  • 0.5 Tsp dried oregano
  • 0.25 Tsp dried thyme
  • 0.5 cup chicken broth
  • 0.5 Lb bacon
  • 16 Oz angel hair pasta
  • 1 Tbsp all-purpose flour
  • 0.5 cup grated parmesan cheese
  • 0.25 cup chipping cream
  • 0.25 cup sun-dried tomatoes
  • 1 pinch red pepper flakes
  • salt to taste
