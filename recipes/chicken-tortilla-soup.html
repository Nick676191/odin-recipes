Chicken Tortilla Soup

Home

Description

This is one of the first soups that I learned to make by myself. I found this recipe on YouTube and it never dissapoints. This is a soup that can be freezed and stored for a long period of time as well.

Ingredients

  • 1 white onion, chopped
  • 1 Jalapeno, diced
  • 4-6 corn tortillas
  • 1 can black beans
  • 1 avocado
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 4 cups chicken broth
  • 1 can crushed fire roasted tomatoes
  • 1 Tsp ground cumin
  • 1.5 Tsp chili powder
  • 0.5 Tsp salt
  • 0.5 Tsp pepper
  • 1 Lb chicken thighs
  • 0.25 cup olive oil

Steps

  1. Heat pot to medium-high heat and add the olive oil and tortilla strips. Wait for it to get hot, remove the crsipy tortilla strips, then add chopped white onion, jalapeno, and garlic
  2. Stir frequently and let the ingredients soften for roughly 4 minutes
  3. Add the chicken broth, crushed tomatoes, ground cumin, chili powder, salt, pepper, and chicken thighs
  4. Stir occasionally and keep the heat up until the mixture starts to boil. Once the mixture starts boiling, move the heat to low and let the mixture simmer to reduce
  5. After the chicken has cooked through (~20-30 minutes), pull out the chicken and shred it
  6. Put the shredded chicken back into the tortilla soup, mix, and serve with shredded cheese, sour cream, and sliced avocado
